Sodexo’s next-generation technology portfolio is revolutionizing campus communities.

Universities are introducing new technologies and strategies to meet this generation’s elevated expectations of quality and efficiency. They understand the benefits of technology and expect it to be integrated into campus life. From tracking occupancy rates in real time by number of cell phones present, to self-driving delivery robots make that local delivery faster, smarter and more cost-efficient, Sodexo is helping to guide its partners with technology solutions that transform campus culture.

Robotic Delivery Service


STARSHIP TECHNOLOGIES


Starship Technologies is the world’s leading autonomous delivery service and operates commercially on a daily basis around the world. Their robots have completed over 25,000 deliveries and travelled more than 150,000 miles. The robots use sophisticated machine learning, artificial intelligence, and arrays of sensors to seamlessly travel on sidewalks and navigate around obstacles. The computer vision-based navigation uses proprietary technology to provide precision in telemetry to the nearest inch. Watch this video to see the robots in action.

The Latest Communication Technology


CHATBOTS ON CAMPUS

Chatbots create a seamless campus experience for users, who can enjoy the convenience of quickly and easily accessing information. With an intuitive user interface that mirrors popular instant messaging formats, Chatbot enables students to engage in a familiar, comfortable manner. In fact, more than 70% of students polled expressed an interest in having a campus Chatbot.  This technology can be integrated to support all aspects of the student journey, from recruitment and enrollment to graduation and beyond.
