In order to achieve student satisfaction, any nutrition services program must not only be experts in food, but truly understand the student customer. That’s why Sodexo has unique programs that focus on students and how to gather their input.

Sometimes their “view” is from a different angle – often a foot or so lower than adults to be exact!
 
We developed an innovative approach to focus groups where students use digital cameras to “capture” our food services from their view.
