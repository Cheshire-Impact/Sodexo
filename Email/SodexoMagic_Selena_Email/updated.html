I am delighted to announce that SodexoMAGIC has named a President to propel the joint-venture between Sodexo and Magic Johnson Enterprise into the future. Selena Cuffe, a successful entrepreneur of a Minority/Women-owned Business Enterprise, brings passion for the SodexoMAGIC mission as well as the leadership experience to strengthen and deliver a compelling value proposition for our clients.

Selena Cuffe
President, SodexoMAGIC

Selena joins SodexoMAGIC with a proven ability to lead teams and grow businesses simultaneously. She has more than 20 years of experience, most notably as co-founder and CEO of Heritage Link Brands, an L.A.-based, MWBE-certified wine enterprise that was driven by her commitment to build partnerships between the Americas and Africa. As CEO, Selena successfully led the brand’s development from concept to sustainable operations, exceeding 70M liters sold annually.
 
Most recently, she served as head of strategy and operations for a Silicon Valley-based food services technology start-up. Selena is a strong marketer having worked in brand management at Procter & Gamble and various roles at the Council on International Educational Exchange, United Airlines, Goldman Sachs and Merrill Lynch. She holds a BA in International Relations from Stanford University and an MBA from Harvard Business School. Cuffe is a member of Delta Sigma Theta Sorority, Inc.
 
Selena will oversee the SodexoMAGIC portfolio, comprised of 1,700 U.S. client sites and 6,500 employees, in partnership with Sodexo’s business lines and senior leaders. 
 
Selena is a lifetime member of the Council on Foreign Relations, served as vice president on Harvard Business School’s alumni board and as a member of the advisory council for Stanford University's Bing Overseas Studies Program. She has been recognized for her work as an entrepreneur and visionary with several awards, including: Woman Trailblazer Award (Brooklyn Library, 2019); Vision in Wine Award (Black Culinary Association, 2016), and B.E. Next "Entrepreneur of the Year” Small Business of the Year Award (Black Enterprise magazine, 2009).
 
Please join me in welcoming Selena. Together, we look forward to our continued partnership.


Sincerely,
Mia Mends
Chief Administrative Officer, Sodexo North America
Executive Vice President, SodexoMAGIC
